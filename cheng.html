Recruitment Process：
We believe that selecting talent is a joint process between Rose Hotel and you. We want to be sure that we find talent that is a match for our teams, and that Rose Hotel is the right match for your values and personal aspirations! Following the formal online application, select candidates will be contacted by our local People and Culture team to coordinate a time for interviews. Throughout the process, you will meet team members through both in-person and virtual interviews.
